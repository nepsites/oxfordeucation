Why Australia?

Top Reasons to Study Here

Global Reputation

8 of Australia's elite "Group of Eight" universities rank in the top 100 globally — a degree from ANU, Melbourne, or UNSW is recognised by employers worldwide.

Post-Study Work Rights

Post-study work options may be available through the Temporary Graduate visa, depending on qualification, eligibility and current rules.

Quality of Life

Australia consistently ranks among the world's most liveable countries. Cities like Melbourne, Sydney, and Brisbane offer safety, diversity, and excellent student life.

Healthcare & Safety

International students on a Student Visa must hold OSHC for the full stay. It helps cover doctor visits, hospital treatment, ambulance and limited medicines.

Requirements at a Glance

What You Need

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • High school or bachelor's transcripts and marksheets
  • IELTS 6.0–6.5 (or equivalent)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Genuine Student (GS) responses/evidence
  • Bank statements showing the current Home Affairs financial capacity amount, plus tuition and travel
  • OSHC (health insurance) receipt
  • University offer letter / CoE
  • Character certificate & police clearance

Visa Process

How to Get Your Student Visa

Get an offer letter
Apply to a CRICOS-registered institution. Oxford International helps with university shortlisting and application documents.
Enrol & pay tuition
Confirm your place by paying the Confirmation of Enrolment (CoE) fee. Your university issues a CoE certificate.
Get health cover (OSHC)
Purchase Overseas Student Health Cover — mandatory for Student Visa 500 applicants.
Apply for Visa 500
Submit your application on ImmiAccount with your CoE, OSHC, passport, financials, and Genuine Student (GS) requirement.
Biometrics & health check
Complete a health examination at a panel physician and provide biometrics if required.
Receive visa & depart
Visa is typically granted within 2–6 weeks. Oxford International provides pre-departure briefing.

FAQ

Common Questions about Studying in Australia

What IELTS score do I need for Australia?
Most Australian universities require IELTS 6.0–6.5 overall, with no band below 5.5 or 6.0. Some postgraduate programs require 6.5–7.0. Oxford International's IELTS coaching has helped hundreds of students achieve their target score.
How much money do I need in my bank account?
Australian immigration requires proof of funds — the current Home Affairs financial capacity amount for living costs, plus tuition and travel. Our visa team helps you prepare the financial documents correctly.
Can I work while studying in Australia?
Yes. Student Visa 500 allows work up to 48 hours per fortnight while your course is in session, and unlimited hours during scheduled course breaks.
What is the Genuine Student (GS) requirement?
The Genuine Student (GS) requirement asks you to show that you are a genuine student, explain your study choice, and provide supporting evidence about your circumstances. Our team helps prepare clear GS responses and supporting documents.
