FAQ

Test Prep Questions

How many mock tests are included?
IELTS students get at least 8 full mock tests throughout the course — one every week or two. Additional mock tests can be arranged on request.
Can I join if my English level is low?
Yes. We first assess your current level and may recommend foundation English classes before IELTS coaching if needed. Many of our best scorers started from basic English.
Do you guarantee a specific score?
We cannot guarantee a specific exam score, as the exam itself is conducted by a third party. However, our goal is to ensure you are thoroughly prepared — and our students typically see significant improvement in their IELTS scores after completing our coaching programme.
Are study materials included in the fee?
Yes — all study materials, practice booklets, and Cambridge IELTS books are provided as part of the course fee. No extra cost for materials.
